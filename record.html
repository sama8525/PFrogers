My record & my plan

A proven track record — thanks to you.

Here’s what we’ve gotten done together on the Pflugerville City Council, and where I want to take Place 6 next.

Since 2020

What we’ve accomplished

  • Defeated the largest tax hike in Pflugerville history. Stopped the Travis County ESD #2 proposal — roughly $350 million over ten years.
  • Lowered taxes and tax rates two years in a row. Real relief for Pflugerville homeowners.
  • Increased the homestead tax exemption 42%. Raised it from $35,000 to $50,000.
  • Launched “Pfetch a Ride.” On-demand, 24-hour rideshare-style transit to replace costly, inflexible fixed-route buses.
  • Upgraded emergency medical service at no cost to taxpayers. Replaced the slower ESD #2 ambulance with faster, high-quality Allegiant Ambulance Service.
  • Expanded parks & trails. More hike-and-bike trails and new Gilleland Park pool facilities.
  • Backed our police. Supported new technology and training for the award-winning Pflugerville Police Department.
  • Improved traffic flow. Opened faster, smarter routes around the city.
  • Brought back Deutschen Pfest. Moved it to October — the biggest and best Pflugerville Pfestival yet.
  • Stood with neighbors through the storms. Helped families recover from the 2021 winter storm and the 2023 ice storm — and demanded accountability and reliability when the lights go out.

These reflect Councilman Rogers’ stated record of votes and initiatives during his time on the council.

Looking ahead

The plan for Place 6

Trust & transparency at City Hall

You deserve a city government that shows its work. I’ll keep pushing for open books, plain-English budgets, and real public input before big decisions are made — not after. No backroom deals, no surprises on your bill.

Rein in spending & pay down debt

Our city’s spending and debt have climbed too fast. I’ll scrutinize every line item, say no to feel-good megaprojects that saddle taxpayers with decades of payments, and put our dollars back into the core services neighbors actually use.

Keep Pflugerville affordable

Between rising taxes, fees, and prices, too many families are getting squeezed. I’ll fight to hold the tax rate down, protect and grow the homestead exemption, and stop the nickel-and-dime fees that quietly pile up on every household.

A city that works for every neighbor

Pflugerville is one of the most diverse towns in Texas — and that’s our strength. I’ll make sure our Black, Hispanic, Asian, and immigrant neighbors have a real seat at the table, and a city that listens to, serves, and invests in every neighborhood.
