My record & my plan

A proven track record — thanks to you.

Here’s what we’ve gotten done together on the Pflugerville City Council, and where I want to take Place 6 next.

Since 2020

What we’ve accomplished

  • Defeated the largest tax hike in Pflugerville history. Stopped the Travis County ESD #2 proposal — roughly $350 million over ten years.
  • Lowered taxes and tax rates two years in a row. Real relief for Pflugerville homeowners.
  • Increased the homestead tax exemption 42%. Raised it from $35,000 to $50,000.
  • Approved the Downtown East project. A public–private partnership replacing the run-down 1984 dentist-office-turned-City-Hall with a vibrant downtown.
  • Broke ground on a major new water plant. Built largely with record federal dollars instead of local tax money — designed to carry Pflugerville well into the future.
  • Launched “Pfetch a Ride.” On-demand, 24-hour rideshare-style transit to replace costly, inflexible fixed-route buses.
  • Upgraded emergency medical service at no cost to taxpayers. Replaced the slower ESD #2 ambulance with faster, high-quality Allegiant Ambulance Service.
  • Expanded parks & trails. More hike-and-bike trails and new Gilleland Park pool facilities.
  • Backed our police. Supported new technology and training for the award-winning Pflugerville Police Department.
  • Improved traffic flow. Opened faster, smarter routes around the city.
  • Brought back Deutschen Pfest. Moved it to October — the biggest and best Pflugerville Pfestival yet.
  • Weathered the storms. Helped Pflugerville recover from the 2021 winter storm and the 2023 ice storm, with new programs and electric & water upgrades to prevent future failures.

These reflect Councilman Rogers’ stated record of votes and initiatives during his time on the council.
