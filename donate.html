For the campaign team — this is the most important setup step. A website can’t legally process political donations on its own. Create an account with a campaign-compliant processor — Anedot is popular for local Texas races (ActBlue / WinRed are alternatives). It collects the donor name, address, employer, and occupation required for Texas Ethics Commission reporting and deposits to the campaign account. Then either:

  2. Link out: point the buttons to your donation page URL.
