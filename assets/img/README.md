## Pflugerville / city photos — IMPORTANT: licensing
Do NOT just save images from a Google search. Most are copyrighted. Use these safe sources:

- Wikimedia Commons (commons.wikimedia.org) — search "Pflugerville". Check each image's
  license; public domain and CC BY are fine. CC BY requires crediting the photographer.
- Portal to Texas History (texashistory.unt.edu) — historic photos, many public domain.
- City of Pflugerville communications office — ask for press/media photos.
- Pflugerville Heritage House Museum — ask permission for historic images and credit them.
- The campaign's OWN photos — always the safest option.

Keep a note of where each image came from and its license, in case anyone asks.

## Recommended sizes
- Headshot / hero: ~1200px on the long edge
- Gallery / blog: ~1000px wide
- Compress with squoosh.app or tinypng.com so pages load fast.
