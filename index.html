A little about me

Not born in Texas… but I got here as fast as I could.

My wife Lisa and I have raised all of our kids in the same Pflugerville house since the day we brought them home from the hospital. I’m a small-town lawyer who stands up for homeowners and neighbors against the folks who’d push them around — and I bring that same scrappy, common-sense streak to the council dais.

When I’m not at City Hall, you’ll find me at a high-school Pfootball game, at the movies, or out back doing battle with the obnoxious weeds in my yard.
